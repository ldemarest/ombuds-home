Peer-to-Peer

Ombuds uses the Bitcoin peer-to-peer protocol. As an epiphyte, Ombuds has a small footprint that interacts with thousands of Bitcoin nodes, but doesn't interfere with the larger Bitcoin ecosystem. Ombuds is well researched, extensively tested, and trusted by organizations and individuals.
