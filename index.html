Peer to peer

Ombuds’ nodes use the well researched and extensively tested Bitcoin peer-to-peer protocol. This network is trusted by organizations and individuals to promptly and securely transfer millions of dollars worth of bitcoin on a daily basis. As epiphytes in the larger Bitcoin ecosystem, Ombuds nodes have a small footprint that don’t interfere with Bitcoin as whole.

Immutable

Ombuds’ broadcasted posts become immutable once they are stored in the Bitcoin block chain. The Bitcoin block chain is distributed globally and inherently public due to economic incentives. Every post will be accessible and cannot be removed by you, by us, or by any authoritarian government.
